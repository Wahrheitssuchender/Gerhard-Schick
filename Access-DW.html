MS Access/VBA

Order/Inventory/Customer Management solution.

Set up for previous employer to handle back-end processes.

SITUATION: The company deals with import and B2B/B2C sales of premium consumer goods.
COMPLICATION: Operations were primarily done manually without any dedicated software to handle orders, product information and customer data. With thousands of SKUs, hundreds of customers, frequently changing prices and promotion sales, back-end processes were extremely slow with errors occuring throughout requiring rework and impacting efficiency and customer service level negatively.
SOLUTION: Moving from MS Excel and Word to MS Access and therefore automating manual processes and keeping track of all relevant business data and statistics.

Among other customized process functions, it includes:

  • Inventory tracking
  • Generation and automated sending of invoices and delivery orders
  • Customer, product and sales data management
  • Data extraction for book-keeping and accounting
