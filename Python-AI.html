Power BI

Operational data analysis and visualization

RISE group project.

SITUATION: A leading car seat manufacturing company produces various items on several production lines and different teams across 30+ countries.
COMPLICATION: Profits have been declining over time because of the steep increase in plants operating costs. Lack of visibility in the production process such as machine downtime, products defect rate, etc. is leading to ineffective operational process in turn increasing costs.
SOLUTION: Creation of an analytics dashboard to track plant's operational process such as downtime minutes, total machine utilization times and other relevant KPIs. For each measure more detailed interactive visuals were provided on additional pages that can be filtered to drill down for further information.

Company Sales Performance and Inventory Overview.

SITUATION: A premium F&B importer and retailer sells wines to mostly private clients among several trade customers. The wines are stored often for several years before they are sold. Clients do pay usually via bank transfer after reception of the wines being delivered to them. All relevant company operational and financial data is managed in MS Access functioning as a CRM and ERP.
COMPLICATION: Data on several KPIs such as sales, inventory levels, customer purchases and product performances are not easily available and visible to make targeted business decisions.
SOLUTION: Reports and a dashboard have been created in PowerBI desktop and shared to allow C-level management gain an overview of the current company status. Interactive filtering allows to drill down and filter out different years, customers and products.
