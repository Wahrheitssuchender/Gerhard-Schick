Microsoft Power Apps

Order, Product and Payment Lists with search functions.

Set up for previous employer to improve sales and marketing operations.

SITUATION: The company deals with import and B2B/B2C sales of premium consumer goods. Stocks are limited and product prices and availability change continuously due to ongoing sales and promotions activities. Customer orders need to be fufilled, delivered and payments be tracked
COMPLICATION: Sales and operations staff have no real time information about product prices and availability, therefore customer orders cannot be confirmed until a physical warehouse check is done or and updated product list is assessed which resides on a server. During peak order times there is no centralized platform to track order status and deliveries can therefore be delayed or missed. Customer payments are not easily visibile to the management and sales staff resulting in accumulated outstanding payments over a long period.
SOLUTION: Creation of several mobile applications with Power Apps that easily allow to check product price and availability, order fulfillment status and outstanding payments. The apps connect to SharePoint lists and Dataverse tables with product, order and payment details which are linked to and updated automatically from MS Access. Search boxes allow to filter the data and linked pages allow to drill down for more details.
