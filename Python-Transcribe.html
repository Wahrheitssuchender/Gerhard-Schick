SITUATION: Generative AI Chatbots are increasingly gaining popularity and are used for providing answers and content in almost all areas of daily life. They are trained on large datasets that span vast topic areas to gain as much general knowledge as possible. of the need for more customized or specialized generative pre-trained transformers (GPT). GPTs are based on a deep learning architecture called the transformer. They are pre-trained on large datasets of unlabeled content, and able to generate novel content. A huge amount of content A leading car seat manufacturing company produces various items on several production lines and different teams across 30+ countries.
COMPLICATION: Information about a very specific topic or which is confidential might not be directly available to the AI model or results can be varying greatly due to conflicting data or plain oversight due to the huge amount of data available.
SOLUTION: Creation of a customized GPT that creates insights and answers primarily from provided data on top of its general trained knowledge. This data is available from printed publications and a big amount of videos. The videos were transcribed automatically with an AI API and the transcripts provided together with other text files to the GPT to create a specialized generative AI.
