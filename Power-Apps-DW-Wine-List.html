Power Apps

Product List with search functions

Set up for previous employer to improve sales and marketing operations.

SITUATION: The company deals with import and B2B/B2C sales of premium consumer goods. Stocks are limited and product prices and availability change continuously due to ongoing sales and promotions activities.
COMPLICATION: Sales and operations staff have no real time information about product prices and availability, therefore customer orders cannot be confirmed until a physical warehouse check is done or and updated product list is assessed which resides on a server.
SOLUTION: Creation of a simple mobile application with Power Apps that allows to check product price and availability instantly. It connects to a SharePoint list with product details which is updated automatically from MS Access after each order creation. Search boxes allow to look up specific products by name, producer, year and price.
