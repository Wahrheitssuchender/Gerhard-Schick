Power BI

Operational data analysis and visualization

RISE group project.

The aim of this project was to analyse and visualize data from a manufacturing plant of a leading car seat manufacturing company which has been seeing a recent decline in operational efficiency of some of its production lines. Following dashboards have been produced that give an overview and show relevant KPIs along with key insights. For each KPI more detailed interactive visuals were provided on additional pages that can be filtered to drill down for further information.
