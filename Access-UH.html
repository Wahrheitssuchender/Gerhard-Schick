MS Access/VBA

Timesheet to capture work done on different projects.

Designed and implemented for an industry client.

SITUATION: The client has a manufacturing line for pharmaceutical packaging machines with several employees involved during the production cycle from procurement to delivery and installation.
COMPLICATION: Exact manufacturing cost calculation for indivisual machines was a challenge as time allocation of people involved in the process could not be captured accurately with the existing tools.
SOLUTION: MS Access multi-user application that allows the team to log time spend on different project tasks. All tasks are consolidated and total allocated time for each project can be displayed, exported and evaluated by team leads and management.
