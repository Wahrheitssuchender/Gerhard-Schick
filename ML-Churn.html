RISE group project.

SITUATION: A major SEA telecom provider is offering services to prepaid and postpaid customer segments combined with variety of product offers and plans.
COMPLICATION: The Company's postpaid business of voice only plans is struggling to maintain its strong foothold in local market because of a high churn rate amongst customers leading to a revenue decline of ~500k USD every month and a decline in overall customer base (high churn rate combined with low acquisition rate), leading to a decline in total market share
SOLUTION: Building of a classification model to predict churners one month in advance and identify key churn drivers. Logistic Regression Model, KNN Classifier and Random Forest Classifier models were tested on test data to find the best perforing one and predict churners on a new dataset. Early prediction of customers in risk of churning saved the company substantial revenue losses.
